# TradeVision AI

## Project Overview

TradeVision AI is an AI-powered intraday stock prediction platform focused on Indian stock markets.

### Markets Covered

- NSE Stocks
- BSE Stocks
- Large Cap Stocks
- Mid Cap Stocks
- Small Cap Stocks
- ETFs

### Prediction Strategy

Update Frequency:
- Every 1 Minute

Prediction Horizon:
- Next 5 Minutes

Prediction Output:
- Buy
- Sell
- Hold
- Confidence Score
- Risk Score
- Target Price
- Stop Loss

### User Types

#### Guest Users

- View stock predictions
- Use market scanner
- Search stocks
- View limited historical performance

#### Registered Users

- Watchlists
- Email Alerts
- Telegram Alerts
- Saved Preferences

#### Premium Users

- Advanced Scanner
- Real-Time Alerts
- Full Historical Analytics
